Linear Algebra

\bigskip
How to orthonormalize $A$, $B$ and $C$.

\bigskip
{\bf 1.}
$$\hat A=|A|^{-1}A$$

\bigskip
{\bf 2.}
$$B^\prime=B-(B\cdot\hat A)\hat A$$
$$\hat B=|B^\prime|^{-1}B^\prime$$

\bigskip
{\bf 3.}
$$C^\prime=C-(C\cdot\hat B)\hat B-(C\cdot\hat A)\hat A$$
$$\hat C=|C^\prime|^{-1}C^\prime$$

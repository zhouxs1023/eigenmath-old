The equation of a circle is $x^2+y^2-4x+6y-12=0$.
Find the center and radius.
\bigskip
The general equation for a circle is
$$(x-a)^2+(y-b)^2=r^2$$
This expands to
$$x^2-2ax+a^2+y^2-2bx+b^2=r^2$$
For the center we have
$$-2ax=-4x,\quad a=2$$
$$-2by=6y,\quad b=-3$$
For the radius we have
$$r^2-a^2-b^2=12,\quad r=\sqrt{12+4+9}=5$$
\end

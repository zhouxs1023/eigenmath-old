{\bf Probability mass functions of the form $p(x)=Cr^x$}

\bigskip
1. An important relation
$$\sum_{x=0}^\infty r^x={1\over1-r},\qquad|r|<1$$

\bigskip
2. Another important relation
$$\sum_{x=0}^n r^x={1-r^{n+1}\over1-r}$$

\bigskip
3. Example
$$p(x)=C(0.25)^x$$
$$C\sum_{n=0}^\infty (0.25)^x={C\over1-0.25}=1
\qquad\Rightarrow\qquad C=0.75$$
$$F(t)=P(X\le t)=1-0.25^{\lfloor t\rfloor+1}$$
$$M(t)={0.75\over1-0.25\,e^t},\quad t<\ln 4$$

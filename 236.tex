Show that $||a|-|b||\le|a-b|$.

\bigskip
1. By the Triangle Inequality we have
$$|a|=|a-b+b|\le|a-b|+|b|$$

\medskip
2. Subtracting $|b|$ from both sides we obtain
$$|a|-|b|\le|a-b|$$

\medskip
3. For $|a|\ge|b|$ we have
$$||a|-|b||=|a|-|b|\le|a-b|$$

\medskip
4. For $|a|<|b|$ we have
$$|a|-|b|=-||a|-|b||<||a|-|b||$$

\medskip
5. Hence $||a|-|b||\le|a-b|$ for all $a$ and $b$.

\noindent
6.3. (b) How would you define the Dedekind cut $-\alpha$
such that $\alpha+(-\alpha)=0^*$?

\bigskip
\noindent
Let $\alpha=a^*$. Then
$$
r\in-\alpha
\longrightarrow
r\in-a^*
\longrightarrow
r\in0^*-a^*
\longrightarrow
r\in(0-a)^*
\longrightarrow
r<0-a
\longrightarrow
r<-a
$$
Hence
$$-\alpha=\{r\in Q:r<-a\}$$

{\bf More trinomial tricks (Note 5 page 18)}

\bigskip
$$p_{Y|X{=}x_0}(y)=C(n-x_0,y)\times \left({p_Y\over1-p_X}\right)^y
\times\left(1-{p_Y\over1-p_X}\right)^{n-x_0-y}$$

\bigskip
$$E(Y|X{=}x_0)=(n-x_0)\times{p_Y\over1-p_X}$$

\bigskip
$$Var(Y|X{=}x_0)=(n-x_0)\times{p_Y\over1-p_X}\times
\left(1-{p_Y\over1-p_X}\right)$$

\bigskip
$$M_{Y|X{=}x_0}(t)=\left(1-{p_Y\over1-p_X}+e^t{p_Y\over1-p_X}\right)^{n-x_0}$$

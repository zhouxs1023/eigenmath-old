\beginsection 3.

Consider the random variables $X$ and $Y$ with j.p.m.f. as
$$p(x,y)={}_{10}C_x{}_{10-x}C_y0.4^x0.3^{10-x},\qquad
x=0,1,\ldots,10,\quad y=0,1,\ldots,10,\quad x+y\le10$$

\beginsection 3. (a)

Find the marginal p.m.f. of $Y$.
\medskip\noindent
Solution: The marginal p.m.f. of $Y$ is the binomial distribution $b(10,0.3)$.
$$p_Y(y)=C(10,y)0.3^y0.7^{10-y}$$

\beginsection 3. (b)

Find the value of the second moment $X$.
\medskip\noindent
Solution: The marginal p.m.f. of $X$ is the binomial distribution $b(10,0.4)$.
From the inside front cover of the book,
$$M(t)=(1-p+pe^t)^n=(0.6+0.4e^t)^{10}$$
$${\partial^2M\over\partial t^2}\bigg|_{t=0}=18.4$$

\beginsection 3. (c)

Find the value of $P(X+Y=3)$.
\medskip\noindent
Solution: Sum over all $p(x,y)$ for which $x+y=3$.
$$P(X+Y=3)=p(0,3)+p(1,2)+p(2,1)+p(3,0)={2{,}250{,}423\over250{,}000{,}000}=0.0090$$

\beginsection 3. (d)

Find the m.g.f. of $Y$ given $X=2$.
\medskip\noindent
Solution:
$$p_{Y|X{=}2}={p(2,y)\over p_X(2)}={C(10,2)C(8,y)0.4^20.3^8\over C(10,2)0.4^20.6^8}
={C(8,y)\over256},\qquad 0\le y\le8$$
Trick alert! This is a binomial distribution with $p=0.5$.
$${C(8,y)\over256}=C(8,y)0.5^y(1-0.5)^{8-y}$$
From the inside back cover of the book,
$$M(t)=(0.5+0.5e^t)^8$$

\beginsection 3. (e)

Find the value of $P(Y{\le}3|X{=}2)$.
\medskip\noindent
Solution: Use a computer.
$$P(Y{\le}3|X{=}2)={p(2,0)+p(2,1)+p(2,2)+p(2,3)\over p_X(2)}=0.3633$$

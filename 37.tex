Compute the following determinant.
$$\left|\matrix{
1&-1&0&1\cr
2&1&-3&4\cr
0&3&-3&2\cr
1&5&4&7\cr
}\right|$$

\bigskip
What we want to do is get a column with just a single 1.
The following row operations do not affect the determinant.

\bigskip
$R_2-2R_1\rightarrow R_2$

$R_3-R_1\rightarrow R_3$

$$\left|\matrix{
1&-1&0&1\cr
0&3&-2&2\cr
0&3&-3&2\cr
0&6&4&6\cr
}\right|$$

\bigskip
We see that the $3\times3$ submatrix has two rows that are the
same so the determinant is zero.

$${}_pF_q=\left[
\matrix{
a_1&a_2&\ldots&a_p\cr
b_1&b_2&\ldots&b_q\cr
};x\right]
=\sum_{k\ge0}{(a_1)_k(a_2)_k\cdots(a_p)_k
\over(b_1)_k(b_2)_k\cdots(b_q)_k}
{x^k\over k!}$$

Example:

$$e^x=\sum_{k\ge0}{x^k\over k!}
=\left[\matrix{
\hbox{---}\cr
\hbox{---}\cr
};x\right]=\sum_{k\ge0}{1\over1}{x^k\over k!}$$
Note: The series ${}_0F_0$ has no $a_n$ or $b_n$ so numerator and denominator
are 1.

2b.
$$p(x)=x/C,\qquad x=1,2,3,4,5$$

\bigskip
Find $C$ so that $p(x)$ is a probability mass function.
$$\sum_{x\in R}x/C=15/C=1$$
$$C=15$$

\bigskip
Determine the distribution function.
$$F(t)=\sum_{x\le t}p(x)$$
$$F(t)=\cases{
0,&$t<1$\cr
1/15,&$1\le t<2$\cr
3/15,&$2\le t<3$\cr
6/15,&$3\le t<4$\cr
10/15,&$4\le t<5$\cr
15/15,&$5\le t$\cr
}$$

\bigskip
Determine the moment generating function.
$$\eqalign{
M(t)&=E[e^{tx}]=\sum_{x\in R}e^{tx}p(x)
=(e^t+2e^{2t}+3e^{3t}+4e^{4t}+5e^{5t})/15\cr
}$$

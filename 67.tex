2. Confidence interval for a proportion, $n=90$, $Y=58$.
$$\hat p=Y/n=0.6444$$
For a 95\% confidence interval $\alpha=0.05$, $Z_{\alpha/2}=1.96$.
$$Z_{\alpha/2}\sqrt{\hat p(1-\hat p)/n}=0.0989$$
The confidence interval is
$$\eqalign{
(\hat p-Z_{\alpha/2}\sqrt{\hat p(1-\hat p)/n}, \hat p+Z_{\alpha/2}\sqrt{\hat p(1-\hat p)/n})
&=(0.6444-0.0989,0.6444+0.0989)\cr
&=(0.5455,0.7433)
}$$

Find the column space of
$$A=\pmatrix{
1&0&-1\cr
1&1&1\cr
-2&1&2\cr
1&0&3\cr
}$$

\bigskip
Solution: Row reduce the columns $A$.
$$\pmatrix{
1&1&-2&1\cr
0&1&1&0\cr
-1&1&2&3\cr
}$$
$R_3+R_1\rightarrow R_3$
$$
\pmatrix{
1&1&-2&1\cr
0&1&1&0\cr
0&2&0&4\cr
}$$
%
$R_3-2R_2\rightarrow R_3$
$$\pmatrix{
1&1&-2&1\cr
0&1&1&0\cr
0&0&-2&4\cr
}$$

\bigskip
We could stop here. Having shown that the original column vectors
are linearly independent, the original column vectors themselves
form a basis for the column space.
However, let us continue to row reduce and get a simpler basis.

\bigskip
$-R_3/2\rightarrow R_3$
$$\pmatrix{
1&1&-2&1\cr
0&1&1&0\cr
0&0&1&-2\cr
}$$

$R_1-R_2\rightarrow R_1$
$$\pmatrix{
1&0&-3&1\cr
0&1&1&0\cr
0&0&1&-2\cr
}$$

$R_2-R_3\rightarrow R_2$
$$\pmatrix{
1&0&-3&1\cr
0&1&0&2\cr
0&0&1&-2\cr
}$$

$R_1+3R_3\rightarrow R_1$
$$\pmatrix{
1&0&0&-5\cr
0&1&0&2\cr
0&0&1&-2\cr
}$$

Transpose...
$$\pmatrix{
1&0&0\cr
0&1&0\cr
0&0&1\cr
-5&2&-2\cr
}$$

The column space is all the linear combinations of the column vectors.
$$\left\{
\pmatrix{a\cr b\cr c\cr-5a+2b-2c}
:a,b,c\in R
\right\}$$

An urn contains eight red balls, five blue balls and seven white balls.
Five balls are randomly drawn, one at a time and WITHOUT REPLACEMENT.
Let $X$ be the number of blue balls drawn, find the variance of $X$.
\medskip
Solution: Number of ``successes'' in a sample of size $n$ from a population
of size $N$ means that we should use the hypergeometric distribution.
$$n=5,\qquad D=5,\qquad N=20$$
$$Var(X)=n\times{D\over N}\times\left(1-{D\over N}\right)\times{N-n\over N-1}
=5\times{5\over20}\times\left(1-{5\over20}\right)\times{20-5\over20-1}=0.7401$$
$$Var(X)=n\times{N_1\over N}\times{N_2\over N}\times{N-n\over N-1}$$
$$\mu=n\times{N_1\over N}$$

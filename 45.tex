1.2 Twenty-seven picowatts of noise with a flat spectrum between 0
and 3 kHz is how many dBrnC?

\bigskip

First calculate dBrn.
$$10\log_{10}\left({27\times10^{-12}\over10^{-12}}\right)=14.3\,{\rm dBrn}$$
Then subtract 2 dB to convert to dBrnC (see page 36).
$$14.3\,{\rm dBrn}-2\,{\rm dB}=12.3\,{\rm dBrnC}$$

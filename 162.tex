{\bf Multiple NEGBINOMDIST}

\bigskip
1. What is the probability that at most five bottles are needed
to have the first bottle contain less than $31.98$ ounces of ale?
$$p=0.3085$$
\medskip
Solution: Sum over all the sequences that lead to success.
$$\matrix{
fail & success \cr
\cr
0 & 1 & \hbox{\tt NEGBINOMDIST(0,1,0.3085) =}&0.3085\cr
1 & 1 & \hbox{\tt NEGBINOMDIST(1,1,0.3085) =}&0.2133\cr
2 & 1 & \hbox{\tt NEGBINOMDIST(2,1,0.3085) =}&0.1475\cr
3 & 1 & \hbox{\tt NEGBINOMDIST(3,1,0.3085) =}&0.1020\cr
4 & 1 & \hbox{\tt NEGBINOMDIST(4,1,0.3085) =}&0.0705\cr
\cr
Total & & & 0.8418
}$$

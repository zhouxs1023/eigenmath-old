1.3 A value of 30 dBrnCO is how many picowatts of absolute noise power
at a $-3$ dB TLP?

\bigskip

See p. 41. TLP stands for transmission-level point.
30 dBrnCO is the noise power at 0 dB TLP.
At $-3$ dB TLP, the noise power is 3 dB lower.
$$30\,{\rm dBrnCO}-3\,{\rm dB}=10\log_{10}\left({P\over1\,{\rm pWC}}\right)$$
$$P=10^{2.7}=500\,{\rm pWC}$$

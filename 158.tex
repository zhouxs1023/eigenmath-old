\beginsection From Quiz 6

The probability density function of $X$ is given as
$$f(x)=\cases{
6x(1-x)& for $0<x<1$\cr
0 & otherwise\cr
}$$
What is the probability density function of $Y=X^3$?
\bigskip
Solution:
$$F(y)=P(Y\le y)=P(X^3\le y)=P(X\le y^{1/3})=\int_0^{y^{1/3}}6x(1-x)\,dx=3y^{2/3}-2y$$
$$f(y)={dF\over dy}=2y^{-1/3}-2,\qquad 0<y<1$$

\beginsection From Review for 2nd Exam

The probability density function of $X$ is given as
$$f(x)=\cases{
\displaystyle{{1\over\theta}e^{-x/\theta}} & for $x>0$\cr
\cr
0 & otherwise\cr
}$$
What is the probability density function of $Y=\ln X$?
\bigskip
$$F(y)=P(Y<y)=P(\ln X<y)=P(X<e^y)
=\int_0^{e^y}{1\over\theta}e^{-x/\theta}\,dx=1-e^{-e^y/\theta}$$
$$f(y)={dF\over dy}={1\over\theta}e^{y-e^y/\theta},\qquad y\in R  $$

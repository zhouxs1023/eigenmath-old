{\bf Bivariate normal distribution}

\bigskip
The general method is compute new $\mu$ and $\sigma^2$ then proceed as usual.

\bigskip
Compute the correlation coefficient $\rho$.
$$\rho={Cov(X,Y)\over\sigma_X\sigma_Y}$$

\bigskip
For $Y|X{=}x_0$ we want to tweak $\mu_Y$
$$\mu=\mu_Y+\rho\left({\sigma_Y\over\sigma_X}\right)(x_0-\mu_X)$$
$$\sigma^2=\sigma_Y^2(1-\rho^2)$$



\bigskip
For $X|Y{=}y_0$ we want to tweak $\mu_X$
$$\mu=\mu_X+\rho\left({\sigma_X\over\sigma_Y}\right)(y_0-\mu_Y)$$
$$\sigma^2=\sigma_X^2(1-\rho^2)$$
